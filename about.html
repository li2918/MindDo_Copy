我们的故事Our Story

从 12 个家庭的实验室
到北美 11 所校区From a 12-Family Lab
to 11 Campuses Across N.A.

2020 年冬天，创始人 Sarah Chen 博士离开 Google Brain，回到尔湾的社区图书馆，给 12 个家庭的孩子上了第一节 AI 启蒙课。那节课上，她意识到一件事：孩子对 AI 的好奇心远超大人的想象，但市面上没有一套真正系统的、对孩子友好的 AI 学习路径。Winter 2020. Founder Dr. Sarah Chen left Google Brain and went back to her Irvine community library to teach the first AI intro class to 12 families. That class made one thing clear: kids are far more curious about AI than adults assume, and nobody was building a truly kid-centric AI learning path.

于是 MindDo 诞生了。我们用了两年时间打磨一套专属 AI 课程体系——不是把大学 CS 课压缩给孩子，而是从"如何正确向 AI 提问"这样的真实场景切入，一步步建立理解、创造与批判性思维。MindDo was born. Over two years we built a curriculum from scratch — not by cramming a college CS course into a kid-friendly shell, but by starting from real scenarios like "how do I ask an AI a good question" and layering up from there.

今天，MindDo 在北美 10 个城市设有线下校区，加上线上课程，服务超过 10,000 名 5–18 岁的学员。我们依然把自己当成一所"精品学校"，而不是"流水线培训班"。Today we run 10 physical campuses across North America plus online classes, serving over 10,000 students ages 5–18. We still think of ourselves as a boutique school, not a factory course.

"真正的 AI 素养，不是会背几个术语，而是遇到一个新 AI 工具时，你能判断它做了什么、能不能信、怎么让它为自己服务。这是我们所有课程的北极星。""Real AI literacy isn't reciting buzzwords — it's looking at a new AI tool and knowing what it does, whether to trust it, and how to make it work for you. That's the North Star for every class we build."

Sarah Chen 博士Dr. Sarah Chen · 创始人 & CEOFounder & CEO

我们的原则Our Principles

六件我们不会妥协的事Six Things We Don't Compromise On

01

每位主讲老师都是有作品的工程师或研究者Every lead instructor is an active builder or researcher

不招只会照着教案念的人。能在业余时间读 arXiv 论文、能用 AI 写出工具的人，才能让孩子真的学到东西。No textbook recitation. Only people who read arXiv on weekends and ship AI tools can teach kids to actually do this.

02

一个班永远不会超过 8 人Classes never exceed 8 students

我们算过：超过 8 人，老师就没法照顾到每个孩子的项目进度。这是底线，从未突破。We ran the math: beyond 8 students, instructors can't shepherd every project. That's a hard ceiling, never broken.

03

每节课都必须产出"可展示"的东西Every class produces something shippable

没有作品的课堂等于没学过。哪怕只是一段 AI 生成的故事，孩子也要能回家给父母展示。A class without an output is a class that didn't happen. Even a one-paragraph AI-generated story — kids have to go home with something to show.

04

家长不需要懂 AI 也能看懂报告Parents never need to understand AI to read our reports

技术名词写在内部文档里就好。给家长看的永远是"孩子能做什么、还能做得更好"。Jargon lives in our internal docs. What parents see is always "what your child can do, and what they could do next."

05

AI 伦理从第一节课就开始讲AI ethics starts from day one

隐私、偏见、AI 幻觉、版权——这些话题不能等孩子"学会编程"之后再谈。应用即伦理。Privacy, bias, hallucinations, copyright — we don't wait until kids "know how to code" to discuss these. To use is to encounter ethics.

06

学费必须配得上家庭的信任Tuition has to justify family trust

我们不做低价拉新、也不做天价吓人。$199/月起的定价，对应的是小班、固定老师和项目产出——家长看得到花在哪。No lowball hooks, no sticker shock. $199/mo starting — buying small classes, dedicated instructors, and real projects. Parents can see where it goes.
