FAQ

家长问得最多的 6 个问题 6 Questions Parents Ask Most

还没找到你的答案？随时 联系我们 或在工作时间内致电校区顾问。 Still have questions? Reach out or call your local campus during office hours.

孩子完全零基础可以吗？My child has zero experience — is that okay?

当然可以。我们 70% 的学员在入学时都是零基础。AI 启蒙入门课程（6–10 岁）从"什么是 AI"开始，用互动工具和小游戏让孩子自然建立认知。Absolutely — 70% of our students start with zero background. AI Fundamentals (ages 6–10) begins with "what is AI" and uses interactive tools and mini-games to build intuition naturally.

每位学员入学都会完成一次 1 对 1 能力评估，我们会根据评估结果安排到最合适的起点。Every student completes a 1-on-1 skills assessment, and we place them at the right starting level accordingly.

和学校 STEM 课程相比有什么不一样？How is this different from school STEM?

学校 STEM 偏重广度覆盖，MindDo 专注 AI 这一条主线，从应用到工程到竞赛一路打通。每一学期都以"可展示的项目"收尾：聊天机器人、图像分类器、AI 故事机等。School STEM covers breadth; MindDo specializes in AI depth — from applications to engineering to competitions. Every term ends with a shippable project: chatbots, image classifiers, AI storytellers, etc.

课程形式是线上还是线下？Are classes online or in-person?

两者都有。我们在北美 10 个城市设有线下校区，也提供小班直播在线课程。线上线下采用完全一致的教材、教师配置和评估体系。Both. We operate 10 in-person campuses across North America and offer small-group live online classes. Identical curriculum, instructors, and assessments across formats.

家长能看到孩子的学习进度吗？How do parents track a child's progress?

家长账户可实时查看课表、每节课后的教师反馈、每 4 周的阶段小结和学期报告。所有数据可随时导出。Your parent account shows live schedule, post-class teacher feedback, 4-week milestone summaries, and semester reports. Everything is exportable at any time.

如果孩子请假或想转班怎么办？What if my child misses a class or wants to change schedule?

提前 24 小时在学员中心提交请假，可安排同周小班补课或 1v1 录播补课。转班需提前一周申请，通常 48 小时内处理完成。Submit a leave request 24 hours in advance through your hub — we'll schedule a same-week makeup or a 1-on-1 recorded session. Class changes require a 1-week notice and typically resolve in 48 hours.
