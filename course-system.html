AI时代孩子成长路径课程体系 Child Growth Curriculum System in the AI Era

以“会用、会思考、会创造、会负责”为主线，围绕认知力、工具力、实践力与社会责任，形成从启蒙到创新的连续课程路径。 Built on the path of using, thinking, creating, and taking responsibility, the curriculum develops cognition, tool mastery, practice, and social ethics from foundation to innovation.

课程主轴：AI素养 + 学科融合 + 项目实战Core: AI literacy + interdisciplinary learning + project practice
培养目标：技术理解、问题解决、表达协作Goals: technical understanding, problem-solving, communication
学习方式：探究任务、作品驱动、反思复盘Learning mode: inquiry tasks, product-driven work, reflection
成果形式：数字作品、研究报告、成长档案Outcomes: digital products, reports, growth portfolios

四阶段成长路径Four-Stage Growth Path

阶段一：AI启蒙（6-8岁）Stage 1: AI Foundation (Ages 6-8) 感知“智能”如何帮助生活，建立规则意识与信息辨识的初步能力。 Understand how AI supports daily life and build early awareness of rules and information quality.
阶段二：AI应用（9-11岁）Stage 2: AI Application (Ages 9-11) 学会使用基础AI工具完成学习任务，强化提问、检索和表达能力。 Use AI tools for school tasks and strengthen questioning, research, and expression skills.
阶段三：AI创造（12-14岁）Stage 3: AI Creation (Ages 12-14) 通过跨学科项目完成从“使用工具”到“设计方案”的跃迁。 Transition from tool users to solution designers through interdisciplinary projects.
阶段四：AI创新（15-18岁）Stage 4: AI Innovation (Ages 15-18) 在真实议题中构建项目，形成责任意识、批判思维与领导力。 Build projects around real-world issues and develop ethics, critical thinking, and leadership.

教学实施节奏（每学期）Semester Delivery Rhythm

  • 第1-3周：主题导入 + 关键能力训练Weeks 1-3: theme kickoff and core skill training
  • 第4-8周：分层任务学习 + 作品小迭代Weeks 4-8: leveled tasks and product iterations
  • 第9-14周：跨学科项目实践Weeks 9-14: interdisciplinary project practice
  • 第15-16周：成果发布 + 成长评估Weeks 15-16: showcase and growth assessment

评估体系Assessment System

形成性评价Formative

课堂参与、任务完成度、合作质量、阶段反思日志。Class participation, task completion, collaboration quality, and reflection logs.

总结性评价Summative

项目成果展示、问题解决过程、表达与答辩能力。Project outcomes, problem-solving process, communication, and defense performance.

成长性档案Portfolio

作品集、能力雷达图、导师评语与下一阶段目标。Portfolio, skill radar, mentor feedback, and next-stage goals.

多元主体反馈Multi-Source Feedback

学生自评、同伴互评、教师评价、家长观察报告。Self-evaluation, peer review, teacher assessment, and parent observations.
