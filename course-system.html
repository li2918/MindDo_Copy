四阶段成长路径Four-Stage Growth Path

阶段一：AI启蒙（6-8岁）Stage 1: AI Foundation (Ages 6-8)

感知"智能"如何帮助生活，建立规则意识与信息辨识的初步能力。Understand how AI supports daily life and build early awareness of rules and information quality.

阶段二：AI应用（9-11岁）Stage 2: AI Application (Ages 9-11)

学会使用基础AI工具完成学习任务，强化提问、检索和表达能力。Use AI tools for school tasks and strengthen questioning, research, and expression skills.

阶段三：AI创造（12-14岁）Stage 3: AI Creation (Ages 12-14)

通过跨学科项目完成从"使用工具"到"设计方案"的跃迁。Transition from tool users to solution designers through interdisciplinary projects.

阶段四：AI创新（15-18岁）Stage 4: AI Innovation (Ages 15-18)

在真实议题中构建项目，形成责任意识、批判思维与领导力。Build projects around real-world issues and develop ethics, critical thinking, and leadership.

评估体系Assessment System

形成性评价Formative

课堂参与、任务完成度、合作质量、阶段反思日志。Class participation, task completion, collaboration quality, and reflection logs.

总结性评价Summative

项目成果展示、问题解决过程、表达与答辩能力。Project outcomes, problem-solving process, communication, and defense performance.

成长性档案Portfolio

作品集、能力雷达图、导师评语与下一阶段目标。Portfolio, skill radar, mentor feedback, and next-stage goals.

多元主体反馈Multi-Source

学生自评、同伴互评、教师评价、家长观察报告。Self-evaluation, peer review, teacher assessment, and parent observations.
