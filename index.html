Control Rules

四个关键控制规则 Four key control rules

支付门禁Payment Gate

首单支付未完成，不允许进入正式排课。No first payment means no formal scheduling access.

老师确认Teacher Confirmation

老师超时未确认，系统自动提醒并重分配。Teacher confirmation timeout triggers reminders and reassignment.

学习风险Learning Risk

缺课、满意度、作业异常自动升级为风险状态。Absence, satisfaction, and homework issues auto-upgrade risk state.

续费节奏Renewal Cadence

剩余课时不足时自动发起续费，沉默则转入召回池。Low sessions trigger renewal automatically; silence routes to win-back.
